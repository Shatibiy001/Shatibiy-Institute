FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What courses do you offer?
We offer a complete range of programs for all ages:
  • Qur’an Reading & Tajweed
  • Hifz (memorization) Program
  • Arabic Language Track
  • Comprehensive Islamic Studies (Fiqh, Aqeedah, Seerah)
Each course is structured into Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced levels to suit your child’s learning pace.
How can my child enroll?
Enrollment is simple! You can register your child through:
  • WhatsApp: direct message us to start
  • Email: send us your child’s details
Once registered, we will guide you on class schedules and materials.
Who is the tutor / founder?
The tutor and founder is Adeleke Uthman (Shatibiy), a certified teacher in Qur’an, Tajweed, and selected Arabic studies. He has completed memorization programs, teaching certificates, and studied extensively in traditional Madrasah curricula. Students benefit from his personal guidance and dedication.
Do you teach different Riwayat of the Qur’an?
Yes! We primarily teach Hafs ‘an ‘Asim, the most common Riwayah, and also introduce Warsh ‘an Nafi‘ for advanced learners. All Riwayat are authentic, and our courses ensure proper pronunciation, correct recitation, and deep understanding.
How long does it take to learn or memorize the Qur’an?
The duration depends on your child’s age, dedication, and selected program. With consistent attendance, structured lessons, and personal guidance, students gradually master reading, Tajweed, and memorization. We provide regular feedback and encouragement to ensure steady progress.
What is a Riwayat?
A Riwayat is a transmission or narration of the Qur’an from the Prophet ﷺ through chains of teachers. Each Riwayat may have small differences in pronunciation or recitation style, but the meaning of the Qur’an remains the same. Learning Riwayat ensures correct and authentic recitation for students.
