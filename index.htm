Who We Are

Authentic Islamic Education,
accessible to every home

Shatibiy Institute of Qur'an & Islamic Studies is a dedicated Islamic E-Learning institute committed to delivering authentic, structured Islamic education to students worldwide. We bridge the gap between traditional Madrasah scholarship and the convenience of modern technology.

Authenticity Discipline Sincerity Excellence Accessibility

Our Vision
To nurture a generation firmly connected to the Qur'an, grounded in authentic Islamic knowledge, and empowered to live by Islamic principles in the modern world.
Our Purpose
Provide accessible, structured, and spiritually enriching Islamic E-Learning that reaches every home regardless of location or circumstance.
The Problem We Solve
Many families lack access to qualified Islamic teachers, face distance barriers, or need flexible scheduling. We eliminate every barrier between learners and authentic knowledge.
What Makes Us Different
Structured curriculum + traditional methodology + certified tutors + personalized attention + flexible scheduling. A complete learning ecosystem.
